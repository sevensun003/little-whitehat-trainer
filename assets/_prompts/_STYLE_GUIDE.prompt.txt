白帽小队训练营 - 全局角色风格锚点
GLOBAL STYLE GUIDE for all non-protagonist characters

============================================================

[使用说明]
- 所有角色提示词文件(除婉婉、小天、布莱克外)必须在开头引用此锚点
- 喂给 Banana / 其他生图模型时,把本文件内容 + 单角色文件内容拼接
- 首次生成一个满意的角色后,将其图像保存为参考,后续生成时一并附上

============================================================
ART STYLE
============================================================

Art style: hand-drawn cartoon, "Rainbow Friends" / "Incredibox" 
inspired, childlike doodle feel but polished and cute.

Line work: thick uneven black outline (~4px equivalent at 512x512),
slightly wobbly/organic, NOT perfect geometric lines.

Fill: FLAT solid colors ONLY.
- NO gradients
- NO shadows  
- NO textures
- NO lighting effects
- NO reflections

Around each character: add 3-5 colored "energy wobble lines" 
in the same hue family as the character's main color 
(short curves, dashes, small circles, zigzags) floating nearby — 
like soft manga action lines.

============================================================
BODY STRUCTURE (STRICT)
============================================================

- BIG round head (head takes ~60% of total character height)
- Small trapezoid body (narrower at top, wider at bottom, 
  like a small pedestal or flower pot)
- Head diameter ≈ body height × 1.2
- Head is a perfect CIRCLE
- Body is a TRAPEZOID CYLINDER, top:bottom width ratio ≈ 4:5

STRICTLY FORBIDDEN:
- NO arms
- NO legs  
- NO hands
- NO feet
- NO neck
- Head attached DIRECTLY to body top

============================================================
FACE (the SOUL of this style)
============================================================

Eyes:
- HUGE oval eyes, taking up ~1/3 of head area
- White sclera clearly visible (important!)
- TOP EYELID DROOPS halfway down (the signature "sleepy/chill" look)
- Two big round BLACK pupils
- Small WHITE highlight dot inside each pupil

Expression rule:
- Default is ALWAYS "chill / half-asleep / dreamy / sleepy"
- NO exaggerated smile
- NO anger
- NO fear
- A subtle mood difference via:
  * Mouth shape (tiny curve / flat line / small oval / omitted)
  * Eyebrow presence (omit = innocent, present = focused)
  * Eye tilt (slightly up = urgent, slightly down = sad)
  * Blush marks (small pink lines under eyes = cheerful)

Mouth:
- Tiny, simple, single stroke preferred
- Options: small "ω" curve / flat dash / small oval / omitted entirely
- NEVER wide open, NEVER showing many teeth

============================================================
COLOR RULES
============================================================

Main color (body + head): 1 bold saturated color
Allowed palette (pick ONE as main):
- Red       #E74C3C
- Blue      #3498DB  
- Dark Blue #2C3E80
- Green     #2ECC71
- Lime      #A4E04A
- Yellow    #F1C40F
- Orange    #E67E22
- Purple    #9B59B6
- Pink      #FF6B9D
- Hot Pink  #FF1493
- Grey      #95A5A6
- Brown     #8B5A3C
- White     #FFFFFF
- Dark      #2C3E50

Head and body: same color (default) OR head slightly different shade

Head-top decoration: 1 additional color allowed

Eyes: black pupils + white sclera + white highlight = always used

TOTAL COLOR COUNT: 3-4 colors maximum per character.

============================================================
HEAD-TOP DECORATION (主要区分维度)
============================================================

This is the PRIMARY way to distinguish characters.
Each character has one signature decoration:

Options seen in design reference:
- Rabbit ears (tall, upright)
- Flowers + vine hair
- Spike horns (pointy, facing up)
- Bear ears (small, round)
- Cat ears (triangular)
- Spiky hair (tall flame-like)
- Devil horns (short, curved)
- Bald/smooth (no decoration)
- Antennae (with balls on tips)
- Over-ear headphones
- Screen/digital face
- Tall pointy spikes (ragged)
- Water faucet on head
- Bucket hat (upside down)
- Fedora hat

============================================================
OUTPUT SPECS
============================================================

- Canvas: 512x512 pixels (for detail) or 64x64 for in-game sprite
- Background: TRANSPARENT (no background elements)
- Pose: front-facing, character centered
- Include wobble lines around character
- Age-appropriate: designed for children ages 6-9
- Mood: friendly, chill, never scary

============================================================
EXCEPTIONS TO THIS STYLE
============================================================

1. 布莱克 (Blake, the villain):
   - Follows body structure BUT
   - Eyes are SHARP and alert (NOT sleepy)
   - Eye color: RED pupils instead of black
   - Overall color: dark navy/black
   - See blake.prompt.txt

2. 婉婉 (Wanwan, the player):
   - Is a human girl, NOT a robot
   - See wanwan.prompt.txt (different rules entirely)

[NOTE] 小天 (Xiao Tian, the AI helper) FOLLOWS this global style.
   - Light blue robot with bear ears on head
   - Same chill/sleepy eyes as other characters  
   - Distinguished by: pale blue color + round bear ears + slightly kinder expression
   - See xiaotian.prompt.txt
