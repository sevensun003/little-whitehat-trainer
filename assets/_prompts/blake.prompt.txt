============================================================
布莱克 (Blake) - 神秘反派(例外:锐利眼神 + 红瞳)
============================================================

[INHERIT] Prepend _STYLE_GUIDE.prompt.txt
[EXCEPTION] Violates the "chill sleepy eyes" rule. Sharp alert eyes instead.

Character: 布莱克 (Blake)
Role: Mysterious villain, revealed later as Xiao Tian's shadow backup AI
Personality: calculating, cold, but not evil in essence — 
  actually a "broken half" seeking completion

Design intent: Distinct from all other characters. 
Must feel slightly threatening but not terrifying (this is for ages 6-9).

============================================================
APPEARANCE
============================================================

Head: big round, dark navy black #1A1A2E (very dark, almost black)
Head-top decoration: 
- NO separate decoration — instead, the head top has
  sharp jagged black spikes radiating upward (like a crown of thorns)
- 5-7 spikes, irregular, uneven (chaotic feel)
- Spikes same color as head (they grow out of it)

Eyes — THIS IS THE KEY EXCEPTION:
- Large oval eyes, same size/shape as other characters
- BUT: top eyelid is LIFTED UP (NOT drooping)
- Pupils are RED #E74C3C (not black) — the one defining color
- Pupils slightly narrowed (more elongated/slit than circular)
- Small sharp white highlight, off-center (like a glint)
- Thin red brow-marks above the eyes (tilted down towards center — 
  "angry/focused" look, but subtle)

Mouth:
- Thin horizontal slit, just a dark line
- NO smile, NO frown — expressionless
- Can be slightly curved DOWN in sinister variant

Body:
- Trapezoid, SAME dark navy #1A1A2E as head
- NO patterns
- Edges slightly "tattered" or uneven (unstable form)

Overall silhouette: should be recognizable as same species as other 
characters but visually "corrupted".

============================================================
COLORS (total: 3)
============================================================

- Dark Navy/Black  #1A1A2E (main body + head)
- Red              #E74C3C (eyes, brow marks — the ONLY accent color)
+ Black & White for outline, eye highlights

Note: Despite being dark, the character should NOT be pure 0x000000 black.
Use dark navy so outlines are still visible.

============================================================
WOBBLE LINES
============================================================

Dark red and dark purple wobble lines — 
SHARPER, more JAGGED, not the soft wobbles of other characters:
- Zigzag marks instead of curves
- Small X shapes
- Jagged lightning-like lines
- Fewer lines total (3-4), giving an "unstable/flickering" feel

============================================================
PHASE-DEPENDENT VARIANTS
============================================================

v1_silhouette.png  - Used in Act 1-2, show as near-pure black silhouette
                     only the red eyes visible, rest indistinct
v2_revealed.png    - Used in Act 3, full details visible
v3_redeemed.png    - Final scene: same shape, but red eyes softening to 
                     calm blue #AEDDFF (echoing Xiao Tian's aura — 
                     visually showing they're the same being)

============================================================
EMOTIONAL TONE CHECK
============================================================

Before finalizing the image, ensure:
- [ ] A 7-year-old would recognize this as "the bad guy" without 
      being SCARED (no teeth, no scary mouth, no dripping effects)
- [ ] The character looks "cool-threatening", not "horror-movie scary"
- [ ] Red is used sparingly — a little goes a long way
- [ ] Still recognizable as belonging to the same "universe" as others

============================================================
OUTPUT
============================================================

- 512x512 portrait + 64x64 sprite
- Transparent background
- Front-facing (v2, v3) or 3/4 shadowy (v1)
- Wobble lines active
